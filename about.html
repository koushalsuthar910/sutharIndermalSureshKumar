Our Story

Since 1985, Suthar Indermal Suresh Kumar has been the cornerstone of quality paint and sanitary solutions in Pindwara, Rajasthan. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name that spans generations.

For nearly four decades, we've been committed to providing our customers with premium quality products and exceptional service. Our journey from a local store to a comprehensive home improvement destination reflects our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.
